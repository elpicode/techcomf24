title: "Step-by-Step Guide to to Create a Knowledge Base for a Team"
date: 2024-11-16T18:26:38+03:00
draft: false
---
A knowledge base helps teams work faster, reduce back-and-forth communication, and easily find the needed information. This guide was created for teams who has not created the knowledge base yet. 

1. **Define the base audience and what problems the base aims to solve.**
2. **Gather audience common issues and recurring questions.**  Focus on practical content that solves real problems.
3. **Set standards for article formatting.** Include
    1. font size
    2. headings and subheadings structure
    3. tone of voice
4. **Create a template for question answers.**
    
    Standardize the format of articles, including:
    
    - Title
    - Problem statement
    - Solving instructions
    - Troubleshooting section
    - Links to related topics.
5. **Write articles with answers to each question.** Ensure that texts are short and precise. 
6. **Ensure that the texts fulfil the standards.**
7. **Break up text with bullet points, numbered lists, and headings** 
8. **Organize articles into categories** 
    - "Getting Started" for new users.
    - "FAQs" for common questions.
    - "Troubleshooting" for resolving issues.
9. **Study how users ask questions and identify frequent expressions and keywords.**
10. **Include keywords to titles and headings** for easier user search.
11. **Pick a platform that allows easy updates, quick navigation, and search options.**
12. **Upload articles to the platform.**
13. **Link related articles to each other by hyperlinks for easier navigation.**
14. **Set up a system to collect user suggestions for next updates.**
15. **Ask a small group to test the base and gather the feedback.**
16. **Improve the articles based on the test results.**
    1. Revise content. 
    2. Fix occurred errors.
    3. Add missing information.
17. **Schedule periodic reviews for maintaining regular updates.** This helps keep the base accurate and relevant.
    1. Analyze  most frequently  accessed articles to identify areas for improvement.
    2. Schedule regular user feedback reviews.
18. **Show the team how to use the base.**
    
    Conduct a training session to:
    1. Assign someone responsible for ongoing maintenance.
    2. Teach how to create and update articles.
    3. Explain navigation and search.
19. Put the link to the base to the main website.
